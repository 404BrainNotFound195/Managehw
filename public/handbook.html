Getting Started
🔐
Create your account
Go to the login page and click the Register tab. Enter your username, email, and a password (min 6 characters). Your account is private — only you can see your tasks.
→ Click "Register" tab → Fill in details → Click "Create Account"
🏠
Explore the Dashboard
After logging in you'll see the main dashboard. The layout has three columns: left sidebar for navigation, center for your tasks, and right sidebar for completed tasks and upcoming deadlines.
➕
Add your first task
Click the orange "+ Add Task" button in the top navbar. Fill in the name, type, priority, subject, and deadline. Click "Save Task" and it appears instantly.
→ Click "+ Add Task" → Fill form → Save Task
2
Features Overview
✅
Task Management
Add, edit, delete tasks. Mark them complete with one click. Tasks are sorted by priority and deadline automatically.
⏱
Study Timer
Time yourself on any task. A floating widget lets you keep the timer running while using the rest of the app.
📊
Study Graph
A graph of your study time appears automatically once you start logging sessions. Tracks the last 7 days.
🍅
Pomodoro Timer
Built-in 25-minute focus / 5-minute break Pomodoro timer in the right sidebar. Counts your pomodoros for the day.
🏷
Custom Subjects & Types
Add your own subjects (e.g. Computer Science) and task types (e.g. Lab Report) using the + button in the task modal.
🔥
Streak Counter
Study every day to build your streak. The flame counter in the navbar shows how many days in a row you've studied.

Priority Levels
🔴 High
Urgent tasks — exams, assignments due very soon. Shown at the top.
🟡 Medium
Normal homework with a reasonable deadline. Default priority.
🟢 Low
Optional reading, extra credit, or long-term projects.
✏️
Edit a task
Hover over any task card — three buttons appear on the right. Click the ✏ pencil icon to open the edit form with all current values pre-filled.
☑️
Complete a task
Click the checkbox on the left of any task card to mark it done. It moves to the "What I've Done" section in the right sidebar. You can undo this anytime.
🔍
Filter & Search
Use the left sidebar to switch between views: All, Due Today, This Week, Overdue, Pending, Completed. Use the search bar in the navbar to find tasks by name. Filter by subject by clicking on a subject in the sidebar.
➕
Add custom subjects & types
When adding or editing a task, click the small orange + button next to "Subject" or "Type" labels. A text field drops down — type your custom option and press Add. Subjects are saved to your account permanently.
→ Open Add Task → Click + next to Subject/Type → Type name → Add

⏱
Open the timer
Click the ⏱ button on any task card, or click the "Set Timer" button in the actions bar. Select which task you're studying for from the dropdown.
▶
Start studying
Press ▶ Start. The modal closes automatically and a floating timer widget appears in the bottom-left corner of the screen — glowing green while running. You can now use the entire app normally.
⤢
Expand or pause
Click ⏸ Pause on the widget to pause (turns yellow). Click ⤢ to reopen the full timer modal. The browser tab title also shows the current time so you can track it from other tabs.
💾
Save your session
When done, click 💾 Save on the floating widget. The time is logged to the task and added to your study history. If you study enough days in a row, your study graph will appear on the dashboard!
→ Time saved → Task updated → Graph appears if data exists
💡
The study graph only appears after you actually log study sessions — it won't show fake data. Once you save your first timer session, the graph will appear automatically on the dashboard.
5
Pomodoro Technique
🍅
What is Pomodoro?
Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5 minute break. Repeat. This technique is proven to improve focus and reduce mental fatigue. Each 25-min session = 1 🍅 pomodoro.
▶
Using it in ManageHw
Find the Pomodoro section in the right sidebar. Press ▶ to start the 25-minute countdown. When it ends, it switches to a 5-minute break automatically. Click 5m to manually switch between focus and break modes.
→ Right sidebar → Press ▶ → Study → Break → Repeat
💡
Use Pomodoro alongside the Study Timer together — start the study timer on a task, then start the Pomodoro countdown. The study timer tracks total time spent, while Pomodoro keeps your focus sessions structured.
6
Pro Tips
🔥
Build your streak
Log at least one study session every day to keep your streak alive. The streak counter is in the navbar — try to keep it growing!
⚠️
Check Overdue view
Click "Overdue" in the left sidebar every morning to see what you've missed. Tasks past their deadline are highlighted in red.
📅
Use "Due Today"
Start each study session by clicking "Due Today" in the sidebar. Focus on what's actually due today before anything else.
🏷
Organise by subject
Add all your subjects at the start of the school year. Filtering by subject lets you focus on one class at a time.
📝
Use notes
The Notes field on each task is perfect for storing page numbers, teacher instructions, or a quick summary of what needs to be done.
🔍
Search fast
Press into the search bar in the navbar and start typing — tasks filter instantly. Great when you have many tasks and need one quickly.
